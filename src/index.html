If the face

If the face is the mirror of the soul. So portrait photography is the canvas where the history of each one of those faces, glances or wrinkles is written forever. Portrait photography, if not the oldest, is one of the oldest disciplines in the field of photography, and perhaps, despite its apparent albeit deceptive simplicity, also one of the most recognized.The photographic portrait was one of the definitive impulses for photography in its beginnings, when it established itself as the natural heir to painting. In fact, like its predecessor, portrait photography began as an exclusive luxury of wealthy families until little by little the advancement of technology made it possible for the majority to immortalize people forever.
